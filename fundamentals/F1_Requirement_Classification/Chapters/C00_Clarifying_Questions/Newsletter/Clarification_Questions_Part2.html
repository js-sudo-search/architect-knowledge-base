✅ Answer Key

Exercise 1 – TinyURL

Suggested Clarification Questions

Is custom alias required? Is login needed? What is the URL lifetime?

Why they matter

These answers define the data model, authentication, and retention policy.

Exercise 2 – WhatsApp

Suggested Clarification Questions

Do we support 1:1 chats, groups, media sharing, end-to-end encryption, and online presence?

Why they matter

These determine fan-out, storage, encryption, and presence architecture.

Exercise 3 – Dropbox

Suggested Clarification Questions

Consumer or enterprise? Version history? Maximum file size?

Why they matter

These influence storage, compliance, synchronization, and retention.

Exercise 4 – YouTube

Suggested Clarification Questions

Uploads only or streaming too? Live streaming? Multiple resolutions?

Why they matter

These drive transcoding, CDN usage, and storage strategy.

Exercise 5 – Uber

Suggested Clarification Questions

Ride hailing only? Real-time tracking? Surge pricing?

Why they matter

These introduce location tracking, matching, and pricing services.

Exercise 6 – Gmail

Suggested Clarification Questions

Attachments? Search? Spam filtering? Offline access?

Why they matter

These affect indexing, storage, and ML capabilities.

Exercise 7 – X (Twitter)

Suggested Clarification Questions

Chronological or personalized feed? Media? Reposts?

Why they matter

These shape feed generation and storage decisions.

Exercise 8 – Netflix

Suggested Clarification Questions

Offline viewing? Adaptive bitrate? Multiple devices?

Why they matter

These determine encoding, CDN, and caching strategy.

Exercise 9 – Google Docs

Suggested Clarification Questions

Real-time editing? Version history? Conflict handling?

Why they matter

These introduce collaboration algorithms and versioning.

Exercise 10 – Amazon Marketplace

Suggested Clarification Questions

Marketplace only? Payments? Inventory? Seller onboarding?

Why they matter

These establish the scope and system boundaries.
