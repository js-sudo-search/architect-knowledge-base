Coaching Record - C05

Learning Objective

Develop architect-level thinking around Non-Functional Requirements.

Major Coaching Scenarios

  1. Conflicting NFRs – User recognized cost vs availability trade-offs and questioned unrealistic requirements.
  2. Food Delivery API – User resisted jumping directly to Redis and first validated assumptions.
  3. Latency Profiling – Learned to localize bottlenecks before optimization.
  4. Photo Upload vs Payment – Distinguished architectures based on business impact.
  5. Communication Coaching – Introduced architect-style articulation.

Reasoning Diagnosis

  • Strengths
    • Business-first thinking
    • Challenges assumptions
    • Considers trade-offs
  • Gaps
    • Initially mixed backend latency with end-to-end latency.
    • Occasionally formed hypotheses before collecting evidence.

Corrections

  • Measure before optimizing.
  • Validate assumptions before selecting technology.
  • Different Functional Requirements have different sets of NFRs.

Outcome

By the end of the chapter, reasoning evolved from solution-first thinking to evidence-driven architectural decision making.
