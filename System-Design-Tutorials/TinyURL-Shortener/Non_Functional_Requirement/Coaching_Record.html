Coaching Record

Your Initial Reasoning

You identified storage, latency, bandwidth, peak traffic, rerouting and availability as NFRs.

Diagnosis

  • Framework Gap: Different architectural stages were being mixed together.
  • Strength: The concerns themselves were valid; they simply belonged to different stages.
  • Storage is an architectural consequence, not an NFR.
  • Bandwidth and headroom belong to capacity estimation.
  • Load balancing and rerouting belong to architecture.

Evolution

You reframed NFRs as quality attributes and identified latency, availability, scalability, durability and reliability without describing implementation.
