Session Insights

Architecture Principles

  • Validate the problem before validating the solution.
  • Frameworks are dependency graphs, not checklists.
  • Reduce uncertainty before making irreversible decisions.
  • Optimize business outcomes rather than individual objectives.

Interview Insights

  • Architects explain why, not just what.
  • Every recommendation should be supported by evidence.
  • Ask questions to eliminate uncertainty.

Common Mistakes

  • Confusing business objectives with proposed solutions.
  • Treating metrics as objectives.
  • Making recommendations before gathering enough evidence.

Learning Capture

The biggest improvement during this session was the transition from isolated statements to causal reasoning using dependencies, business value, constraints and trade-offs.
